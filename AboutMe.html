About Me











My name is Matthew and I am a student at Stuyvesant High School. I live in New York City and previously attended New Explorations into Science, Technology, and Math(NEST+M). My favorite foods are Vietnamese Pho, Ramen, and French Fries/Curly Fries/Onion Rings. In terms of music, I enjoy most compositions with a good bass(dubstep, upbeat remixes, etc). I have danced for the past 4 years of my life at National Dance Institue(NDI), in Harlem, and have been doing musical theatre since elementary school at the Junior Theatre Festival(JTF) with my school's theatre partnership with the National Asian Artists Project(NAAP). I am currently in a coding and robotics program called Kinet-X that has been very interesting and informational.
